// Common Questions
FAQ
How fast can you build my website?
Standard websites are delivered in 48–72 hours from when you send us your content. More complex multi-page projects take 3–5 days. We’ve never missed a deadline.
Do you use WordPress or Wix?
Never. Every website is built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no page builders, no CMS, no templates. This gives us complete control over design, performance, and animations.
Will my site look good on mobile?
Absolutely. We design mobile-first, meaning your phone layout is built before the desktop version. We test on multiple screen sizes and devices before every delivery.
What do I need to get started?
Just your business name, a description of what you offer, any brand colors or fonts you use, and the content you want on the site. We guide you through everything else — no tech experience needed.
Can you update my site after launch?
Yes — we offer ongoing maintenance and update services. Whether you need to change menu items, update prices, add new sections, or fix anything, we handle it quickly. Most updates are done same-day.
Do you work outside Lebanon?
Yes, 100% remote and worldwide. We’ve worked with clients in Lebanon, the Gulf, Europe, and beyond. Communication happens over WhatsApp or email — wherever you are, we can work with you.
